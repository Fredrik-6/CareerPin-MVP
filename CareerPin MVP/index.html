Career Roadmap

Data Analyst

This roadmap is designed to help students or graduates take their first steps toward becoming a Data Analyst. It starts by introducing key concepts in data analytics and suggests building knowledge in tools like Excel, SQL, and Python. These tools are widely used by companies to organise and analyse data.

Once you’ve gained some basic skills, the next step is to apply them through hands-on projects. These projects can show potential employers that you can solve real-world problems. Building a portfolio with your best work is a great way to demonstrate your abilities.

The roadmap also highlights the importance of networking with professionals and improving communication skills to present your insights effectively. By following these steps, you’ll be prepared to apply for entry-level Data Analyst roles and start your career with confidence.
